# 2H3+

The D₃⁺ isotopologue of H₃⁺ (fully deuterated). The [23BoMiZo](../Papers/23BoMiZo.md) paper presents the new **MiZo** line list for this species, computed with DVR3D. Unlike the other three H₃⁺ isotopologues, D₃⁺ uses effective Hamiltonian (EH) energies derived from molecular constants rather than MARVEL, due to insufficient published laboratory spectral data to form a well-connected network. Sources: [23BoMiZo](../../Raw/Sources/23BoMiZo.json), [24TeYuZh](../../Raw/Sources/24TeYuZh.json).

D₃⁺ has no MARVEL network due to insufficient laboratory data. Energies for J=0–15 up to 2,676 cm⁻¹ were computed from effective Hamiltonian constants (PGOPHER). A further 1,045 states at higher energies were assigned manually using published vibrational band origins. The line list is best suited for infrared studies; pure-rotational frequency predictions carry higher uncertainty than for the other three isotopologues. 43 meta-stable states have radiative lifetimes exceeding 10¹⁰ s. Note: the 24TeYuZh ExoMol database release marked D₃⁺ as `is_marvelized: true`; the definitive [23BoMiZo](../Papers/23BoMiZo.md) line list paper clarifies that no MARVEL energies are used.
