## Line List

**NaOUCMe** ([22MiTaTe](../Papers/22MiTaTe.md)) — rovibronic line list for 23Na-16O covering the X²Π and A²Σ⁺ electronic states (0–10,000 cm⁻¹, T_max = 2500 K). Variational coupled-channel calculation using Duo with MRCI/aug-cc-pVQZ ab initio PECs and non-adiabatic coupling functions (MOLPRO), refined against rotational and A–X rovibronic experimental data. Contains 36,120 rovibronic levels and 4,726,137 dipole-allowed transitions. No MARVEL procedure applied; energies are fully variational with experimental refinement via Duo least-squares fitting.

A state excitation energy ~1982 cm⁻¹ (mid-IR); vibrationally averaged X state permanent dipole moment 8.44 D. State-dependent energy uncertainties provided following the ExoMol standard.
