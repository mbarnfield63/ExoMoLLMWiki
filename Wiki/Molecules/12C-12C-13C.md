# 12C-12C-13C

Asymmetric ¹³C-substituted isotopologue of tricarbon (¹²C¹²C¹³C), with ¹³C at one end. Cₛ symmetry; the absence of nuclear-spin selection rules (¹²C nuclear spin = 0) means all parity states are present, yielding the largest transition count of the three C₃ isotopologues.
