### Computation

- **PES**: CCSD(T)/aug-cc-pCV6Z composite ab initio (previously published); core-valence correlation, higher-order CC terms, scalar relativistic effects, diagonal Born-Oppenheimer correction included. Tenth-order polynomial expansion in valence coordinates (44 parameters).
- **DMS**: newly computed at CCSD(T)/aug-cc-pCVQZ (Li, O) + aug-cc-pVQZ (H) using MOLPRO2015 on 274-point grid. Sixth-order polynomial fits: Aₓ (72 params), Aᵧ (64 params).
- **Nuclear motion**: variational calculation with Sinc-DVR basis (100 functions, Li-O: 2.0–7.0 bohr; O-H: 1.1–6.0 bohr) + 120 Legendre polynomials (bending); Hamiltonian dimension 10,000 for vibrational step; full rovibrational diagonalisation up to J=95.
