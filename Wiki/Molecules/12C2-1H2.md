## Line List

**aCeTY** ([20ChTeYu](../Papers/20ChTeYu.md)) — ro-vibrational line list for the ground electronic state of ¹²C₂H₂ (HCCH, acetylene). Computed with TROVE using a VQZ-F12/CCSD(T)-F12c PES (refined to MARVEL energies) and a CCSD(T)/aug-cc-PVQZ DMS. Vibrational transition dipole moments scaled band-by-band against HITRAN-2016. MARVELised energies replace calculated values where available. Contains **4,347,381,911 transitions** between **5,160,803 energy levels**, covering 0–10,000 cm⁻¹ (≥ 1 µm), complete to ~2200 K, J_max = 99, l_max = 16.

The aCeTY line list uses MARVEL empirical energy levels from a 2017 MARVEL analysis of all published ¹²C₂H₂ spectroscopic data to replace calculated Duo values in the states file and to refine the PES. The total number of MARVEL energy levels incorporated is not stated in the source paper. Source: [20ChTeYu](../../Raw/Sources/20ChTeYu.json).
