# 26Al-1H

Minor isotopologue of aluminium hydride containing the radioactive ²⁶Al nucleus (t½ ≈ 710 000 yr). [23YuSzHa](../Papers/23YuSzHa.md) AloHa line list produced by applying the ²⁷AlH spectroscopic model to the ²⁶Al mass; no independent experimental data or MARVEL analysis is available for this isotopologue. ²⁶Al has been clearly detected in the Milky Way.
