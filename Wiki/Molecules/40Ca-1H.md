## Methods

Variational rovibronic calculations performed with **Duo** using a 501-point sinc-DVR grid (range 1.0–6.0 Å). Spectroscopic model includes spin-orbit (X-A, X-B, A-B), electronic angular momentum (X-A, X-B), Born-Oppenheimer breakdown, spin-rotation, and Λ-doubling couplings. A-B and X-B spin-orbit and angular momentum couplings computed ab initio at MCSCF/cc-pCVQZ (Ca) + cc-pVQZ (H) level with MOLPRO2015 and morphed to MARVEL data. CaH requires a "dummy" B′²Σ⁺ state to model the avoided crossing in the B state near Ca-H bond length ~3.2 Å. MARVEL refinement reproduced 1,170 term values with w-rms = 0.001 cm⁻¹ and rms = 0.241 cm⁻¹. Landé g-factors computed for all energy levels for stellar magnetic-field diagnostics.
