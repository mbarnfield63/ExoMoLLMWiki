### Computation

- **Ab initio**: ic-MRCI/aug-cc-pV5Z; 12 active electrons; occupied (8,3,3,0)/closed (4,1,1,0) under C2v symmetry; 13 electronic states computed; diabatic representation built by removing avoided crossings between adiabatically degenerate pairs.
- **Nuclear motion**: [Duo](../Methods/Duo.md) variational code; sinc-DVR basis on 301-point grid (0.6–6.0 Å); 58 vibrational wavefunctions per state forming contracted vibronic basis.
- **Potential energy curves**: Extended Morse Oscillator (EMO) analytical form; X³Σ⁻ dissociation asymptote S(³P) + O(³P) at 5.42895 eV.
- **Dipole moment curves**: ground-state DMS fitted to "irregular DMC" Chebyshev form (13 parameters), scaled to 1.535 D; A³Π transition dipole scaled from experimental A-state lifetime; forbidden-band intensities from intensity-stealing via SOCs and EAMCs.
- **Empirical corrections**: rotational Born-Oppenheimer breakdown (BOB), spin-spin (SS), and spin-rotation (SR) curves for triplet states.
