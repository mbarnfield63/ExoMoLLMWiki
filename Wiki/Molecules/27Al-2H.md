## Evidence

New FT-VIS emission spectra of ten AlD A¹Π–X¹Σ⁺ bands — including (0-0), (0-1), (0-2), (1-0), (1-1), (1-2), (1-3), (1-4), (2-1), (2-2) — recorded with a Bruker IFS-125HR Fourier transform spectrometer at the University of Rzeszów (resolution 0.03 cm⁻¹, SNR ~4000:1 for strongest band). The (2-1) and (2-2) bands are reported for the first time. The AlD B¹Σ⁺ predissociative band (missing entirely in WYLLoT) is now included; the diabatic model of this shallow state holds only three bound vibrational levels in AlD. Predissociative lifetimes computed via Duo's semiclassical procedure are included in the ExoMol States file alongside radiative lifetimes. Source: [23YuSzHa](../../Raw/Sources/23YuSzHa.json).
