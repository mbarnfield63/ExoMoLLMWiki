## Line List

**SiOUVenIR** ([22YuTeSy](../Papers/22YuTeSy.md)) — IR and UV rovibronic line list for ²⁸Si¹⁶O covering nine electronic states: X¹Σ⁺, A¹Π, E¹Σ⁺, C¹Σ⁻, D¹Δ, a³Σ⁺, b³Π, e³Σ⁻, and d³Δ. Contains **91,395,763 transitions** and **174,250 states** covering wavenumbers up to 72,000 cm⁻¹ (λ > 140 nm) for J = 0–250, valid up to 10,000 K. Ab initio PECs, SOCs, and EAMCs computed at IC-MRCI-F12c/QZ-F12 level with MOLPRO2015, empirically refined with Duo against MARVEL energies. Published ab initio TDMCs (Bauschlicher 2016) used for the E–X and A–X bands. Supersedes the EBJT rotation-vibration line list (13BaYuTe). MARVELised energies replace calculated Duo values where available. Partition functions (to 10,000 K), Landé g-factors, state uncertainties, and radiative lifetimes provided.
