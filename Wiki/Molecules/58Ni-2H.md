# 58Ni-2H

Deuterated isotopologue of nickel monohydride (⁵⁸NiD). [24BaYuOw](../Papers/24BaYuOw.md) BYOT line list computed using the Duo variational code with a separately built empirical spectroscopic model; calculated energy levels replaced with empirical values. The Duo model required additional refitting compared to the NiH isotopologues (refit of diagonal SOCs and addition of diabatic diagonal correction curves) due to limited experimental coverage and larger rms errors. Source: [24BaYuOw](../../Raw/Sources/24BaYuOw.json).

## Evidence

Separate Duo model built for 58NiD; quality of fit decreased by two orders of magnitude relative to NiH isotopologues due to more limited experimental coverage. Requires refit of diagonal SOC curves and addition of diabatic correction curves. Deuterium abundance assumed proportional to Jupiter D/H ratio for abundance-adjusted spectra. Isotopic mass change gives larger frequency shifts and an intensity reduction (larger partition function than NiH at the same temperature). Source: [24BaYuOw](../../Raw/Sources/24BaYuOw.json).
