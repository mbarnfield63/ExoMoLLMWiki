## Line List

**SiNful** ([22SeClYu](../Papers/22SeClYu.md)) — empirical rovibronic line list for 28Si-14N covering the six lowest electronic states (X²Σ⁺, A²Π, B²Σ⁺, a⁴Σ⁻, b⁴Π, c⁴Σ⁺) up to 58,000 cm⁻¹. Contains 131,935 states and 43,646,806 transitions. Ab initio PECs, SOCs, EAMCs, and DMCs computed at MRCI/aug-cc-pVQZ with MOLPRO, empirically refined with Duo against MARVEL and PGOPHER term values. MARVELised energies replace calculated Duo values where available. Partition functions, Landé g-factors, and state uncertainties provided.
