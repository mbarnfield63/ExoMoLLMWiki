## Methods

Variational rovibronic calculations performed with **Duo** using a 401-point sinc-DVR grid (range 1.0–5.0 Å). The X²Σ⁺ MLR potential function from prior literature was refined, with Born-Oppenheimer breakdown and spin-rotation coupling terms added. The B²Σ⁺ PEC uses an empirically determined RKR curve morphed in refinement. Ab initio couplings at MCSCF/cc-pCVQZ (Mg) + cc-pVQZ (H) level were computed with MOLPRO2015 and morphed to MARVEL data. MARVEL refinement reproduced 1,827 energy levels with w-rms = 0.019 cm⁻¹ and rms = 0.262 cm⁻¹. Lower state energy threshold set to include quasibound X-state levels; upper state threshold at 30,000 cm⁻¹. Landé g-factors computed for stellar magnetic-field diagnostics.
