## Evidence

AloHa line list computed with Duo using a diabatic model of the shallow B¹Σ⁺ state (two quasi-bound vibrational levels in AlH, v=0 and v=1). Model refined by fitting to MARVEL empirical energies derived from 16 experimental sources (rms 0.08 cm⁻¹ over 346 levels). The X¹Σ⁺ PEC and B¹Σ⁺ model were constrained to the AlD solution and then re-refined for AlH with 8 free parameters. Predissociative lifetimes computed via Duo's semiclassical procedure are included in the States file alongside radiative lifetimes. New line list reproduces heavily predissociated lines in the Proxima Cen spectrum (HARPS archive) that WYLLoT could not describe. Also listed in the ExoMolHR contents table (24TeYuZh Table 17).
