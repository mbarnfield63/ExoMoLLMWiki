## Line List

**OYT3** — ExoMol XXXVIII ([20OwCoTe](../Papers/20OwCoTe.md), 2020). First comprehensive rovibrational line list for SiO2. ## Evidence

OYT3 presented in [20OwCoTe](../Papers/20OwCoTe.md): CCSD(T)-F12b/CBS PES and DMS on 15,365-point grid, TROVE (linear triatomic extension), benchmarked with DVR3D. 32,951,275,437 transitions, 5,688,942 levels, 0–6,000 cm⁻¹, T_max 3000 K (soft limit). No experimental data available for empirical refinement. Source: [20OwCoTe](../../Raw/Sources/20OwCoTe.json).
