# petitRADTRANS

petitRADTRANS is an atmospheric radiative transfer code used for exoplanet atmosphere modelling and spectral simulation. The Dozen CO2 line list paper generated ExoMolOP opacity tables compatible with petitRADTRANS. Source: [26YuBaBo](../../Raw/Sources/26YuBaBo.json).
