# ExoMol Database

The 2024 ExoMol database release describes ExoMol as a molecular data resource for spectroscopic studies of hot atmospheres. Source: [Tennyson, Yurchenko, and Zhang et al. 2024](../../Raw/Sources/24TeYuZh.xml).

The release reports recommended line lists for 91 molecules and 224 isotopologues, with a total of almost 10^12 individual transitions. Source: [Tennyson, Yurchenko, and Zhang et al. 2024](../../Raw/Sources/24TeYuZh.xml).

The paper identifies new database features including ultraviolet line-list extensions, photodissociation cross-sections, extended broadening parameters, JSON data specifications, specific heats, plasma-study lifetimes, and the ExoMolHR web app. Source: [Tennyson, Yurchenko, and Zhang et al. 2024](../../Raw/Sources/24TeYuZh.xml).
