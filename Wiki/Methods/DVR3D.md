# DVR3D

DVR3D is a variational nuclear-motion program suite that computes rotation-vibration energy levels and Einstein A-coefficients for triatomic molecules using a discrete variable representation (DVR) approach. In the [23BoMiZo](../Papers/23BoMiZo.md) ExoMol L paper, DVR3D was used for variational nuclear motion calculations of H₃⁺ and its isotopologues; a new LINELIST module was added to convert DVR3D output to ExoMol format. Source: [23BoMiZo](../../Raw/Sources/23BoMiZo.json).
