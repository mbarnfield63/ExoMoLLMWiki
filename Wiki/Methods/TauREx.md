# TauREx

TauREx is an atmospheric retrieval and radiative transfer code developed within the ExoMol group for exoplanet atmosphere analysis. The Dozen CO2 line list paper generated ExoMolOP opacity tables compatible with TauREx. Source: [26YuBaBo](../../Raw/Sources/26YuBaBo.json).
