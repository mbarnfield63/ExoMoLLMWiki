# Alec Owens

Alec Owens is lead author of the ExoMol XLV CaH and MgH XAB rovibronic line lists paper, lead author of the ExoMol LI LiOH OYT7 line list paper, co-author of the ExoMol XLIV SiO SiOUVenIR line list paper, co-author of the ExoMol LX ¹⁵NH₃ CoYuTe-15 line list paper, co-author of the ExoMol LXV NiH BYOT line list paper, and co-author of the 2024 ExoMol database release.
