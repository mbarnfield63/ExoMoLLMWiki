# Anna-Maree Syme

Anna-Maree Syme (UNSW) is co-author of the ExoMol XLIV SiO SiOUVenIR line list paper and co-author of the hybrid diatomic line list paper (24McBoKe) covering updated ExoMol line lists for MgO, TiO, and VO.
