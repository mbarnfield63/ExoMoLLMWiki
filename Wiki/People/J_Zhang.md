# Jingxin Zhang

Jingxin Zhang (UCL) is first author of the ExoMolHR high-resolution empirical spectral database paper, co-author of the ExoAtom atomic spectroscopy database paper, co-author of the ExoMol LX ¹⁵NH₃ CoYuTe-15 line list paper, and co-author of the 2024 ExoMol database release.
