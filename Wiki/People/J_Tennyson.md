# Jonathan Tennyson

Jonathan Tennyson (UCL) is lead author of the 2024 ExoMol database release, co-author of the ExoMol LX ¹⁵NH₃ CoYuTe-15 line list paper, co-author of the N₂O TYM line lists paper, and co-author of the Dozen CO₂ line lists paper.
