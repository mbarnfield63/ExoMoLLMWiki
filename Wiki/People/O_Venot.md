# Olivia Venot

Olivia Venot (Université Paris Cité / CNRS, LISA) is co-author of the ExoPhoto database paper, representing the EXACT database (EXoplanetary Atmospheric Chemistry at High Temperature) which contributed experimental photodissociation cross sections for CO₂ and C₂H₂.
