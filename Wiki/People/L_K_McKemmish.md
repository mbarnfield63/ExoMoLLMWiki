# Laura K. McKemmish

Laura K. McKemmish is lead author of the ExoMol XXXIII TiO Toto line list paper (19McMaJe) and lead author of the hybrid diatomic line list paper (24McBoKe) presenting updated ExoMol line lists for MgO, TiO, and VO, and co-author of the 2024 ExoMol database release. Sources: [19McMaJe](../../Raw/Sources/19McMaJe.json), [24McBoKe](../../Raw/Sources/24McBoKe.json), [24TeYuZh](../../Raw/Sources/24TeYuZh.json).
