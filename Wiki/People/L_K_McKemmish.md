# Laura K. McKemmish

Laura K. McKemmish is lead author of the hybrid diatomic line list paper (24McBoKe) presenting updated ExoMol line lists for MgO, TiO, and VO, and co-author of the 2024 ExoMol database release. Sources: [24McBoKe](../../Raw/Sources/24McBoKe.json), [24TeYuZh](../../Raw/Sources/24TeYuZh.json).
