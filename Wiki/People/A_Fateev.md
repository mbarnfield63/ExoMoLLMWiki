# Alexander Fateev

Alexander Fateev (DTU) is co-author of the ExoPhoto database paper. The DTU group contributed experimental far-UV photodissociation cross section measurements for CO, CO₂, H₂O, H₂CO, NH₃, and C₂H₄, published here for the first time in ExoPhoto.
