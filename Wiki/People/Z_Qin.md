# Zhi Qin

Zhi Qin (Shandong University) is co-author of the ExoPhoto database paper, representing the PhoMol project which contributed calculated temperature-dependent photodissociation cross sections for AlCl, AlF, AlH, HCl, HF, MgO, NaO, and O₂.
