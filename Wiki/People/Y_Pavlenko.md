# Yakiv Pavlenko

Yakiv Pavlenko (Main Astronomical Observatory, Kyiv, Ukraine) is co-author of the ExoMol LI LiOH OYT7 line list paper and co-author of the ExoMol LIV AloHa AlH/AlD line lists paper (where he contributed astrophysical measurements of AlH in the Proxima Cen spectrum as MARVEL input, under the MSCA4Ukraine programme).
