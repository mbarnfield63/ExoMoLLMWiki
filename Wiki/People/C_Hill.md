# Christian Hill

Christian Hill (IAEA, Vienna) is co-author of the 2024 ExoMol database release, the ExoAtom atomic spectroscopy database paper, the ExoMolHR high-resolution empirical spectral database paper, and the ExoPhoto photodissociation cross-section database paper. Hill contributes to ExoMol's web infrastructure and database management.
