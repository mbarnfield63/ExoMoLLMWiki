# Marco Pezzella

Marco Pezzella (Università di Perugia, formerly UCL) is co-author of the ExoPhoto photodissociation cross-section database paper, the ExoMol L H₃⁺ family line lists paper, the ExoMol LX ¹⁵NH₃ CoYuTe-15 line list paper, and the 2024 ExoMol database release.
