# Charles A. Bowesman

Charles A. Bowesman is lead author of the ExoMol L H₃⁺ family line lists paper, co-author of the hybrid diatomic line list paper (MgO, TiO, VO), the ExoMol LX ¹⁵NH₃ CoYuTe-15 line list paper, the 2024 ExoMol database release, and the Dozen CO₂ line lists paper.
