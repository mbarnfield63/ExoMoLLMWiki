# 22SeClYu

[22SeClYu](../Papers/22SeClYu.md) — Semenov, Clark, Yurchenko, Kim & Tennyson (2022), *MNRAS*.

## Summary

ExoMol XLVI presents **SiNful**, empirical rovibronic line lists for four isotopologues of silicon mononitride: [28Si-14N](../Molecules/28Si-14N.md), [29Si-14N](../Molecules/29Si-14N.md), [30Si-14N](../Molecules/30Si-14N.md), and [28Si-15N](../Molecules/28Si-15N.md). The line lists cover the six lowest electronic states — X²Σ⁺, A²Π, B²Σ⁺, a⁴Σ⁻, b⁴Π, c⁴Σ⁺ — from infrared through ultraviolet up to 58,000 cm⁻¹.

Ab initio PECs, SOCs, EAMCs, and DMCs were computed at the MRCI/aug-cc-pVQZ level using MOLPRO2020, then empirically refined using Duo against MARVEL and PGOPHER energy levels. The MARVEL procedure for 28Si-14N processed 1987 experimental and 9 pseudo-experimental transitions from 7 literature sources (1929–1992), producing 1054 empirical energy levels and 1456 validated transitions in one main spectroscopic network, covering the X–X, A–X, and B–X band systems up to 30,308 cm⁻¹. MARVELised energies replace Duo-calculated values where available. Minor isotopologue line lists (29Si-14N, 30Si-14N, 28Si-15N) are generated by mass-scaling the Duo model. Partition functions (up to 10,000 K), Landé g-factors, state uncertainties, and radiative lifetimes are provided. Source: [22SeClYu](../../Raw/Sources/22SeClYu.json).
